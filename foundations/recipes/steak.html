Pan-seared Steak

Description

With this recipe you'll be able to cook a delicious restaurant-quality steak at home for a cheaper price and a better taste.

This is a recipe that takes very little time in terms of cooking!

Ingredients

  • Frying pan (Stainless steel, Cast iron, Carbon steel are preferable)
  • Your choice of steak (I recommend Ribeye!)
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Aromatics of your choice (Rosemary, thyme, garlic, etc)

Steps

  1. Dry brine the steak (1.5% weight of steak) for at least 1 hour, up to 24 hours in advance
  2. Pre-heat the frying pan for 5 minutes or so
  3. Add 1 or 1.5 Tbps olive oil, it should slightly smoke to let you know the temperature is ready for searing
  4. Add steak to pan and get a good sear on each side
  5. After searing, flip the steak every 15 seconds to ensure even cooking
  6. At this point, add your aromatics and butter
  7. Cook to your desired doneness, then let the steak rest for 5 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
