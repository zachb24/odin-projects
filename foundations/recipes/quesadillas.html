Quesadillas

Description

Quesadillas are a classic Mexican dish that primarily consist of a corn or flour tortilla, cheese, and sometimes a protein.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Cheese
  • Optionally: Protein (chicken, beef, etc)

Steps

  1. Place tortillas in a flat manner in the frying pan on low-medium heat
  2. Check for browning after a few minutes, and then flip all quesadillas
  3. Place cheese on tortillas, as well as any chosen proteins and seasonings
  4. Immediately fold tortillas into a semicircle shape
  5. Brown both sides of tortilla, and then remove from pan
  6. Enjoy!
